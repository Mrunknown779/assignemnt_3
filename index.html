About Skittles

Skittles is a popular fruit-flavored candy known for its vibrant colors and delicious taste.

Cool Facts About Skittles

  • Skittles were first invented in 1974 in England.
  • They were introduced in the United States as an imported product in 1979.
  • Skittles is sold to more than 60 countries around the world.
  • The original Skittles has 5 colors.
  • For every 100 grams, Skittles contain 400 calories.

History of Skittles

Skittles were first made commercially by Mars Candy Company in 1974. They first came to the United States as an import confectionery in 1979. Local production in the U.S. started in 1982.
